    • About Htoo Eain Tin

    Htoo Eain Thin was one of the most popular and respected Burmese singer-songwriters. He brought a new style of pop rock music to the Burmese music scene in mid-1980s, and was popular till his death in 2004. He released 14 solo albums in his 18-year career.At here we present an incredible compilation of Htoo Eain Tin music videos and songs with lyrics that you would truly enjoy!
